  The German Football Association (DFB) is the head of football in Germany and is one of the founding members of the FIFA and UEFA. It includes many football associations and football clubs. The headquarter of the DFB is in Frankfurt am Main. It has also lots of members and is therefore, the greatest national sports association. As the national football association it also organizes the national teams for women and men, in case of players and coach.

 The app should be used for organizing and manage the structures of the DFB. It will therefore, have the task for handling the football associations of the DFB and the associated football clubs with people who are associated in them. Moreover, the app manages the information of national teams for women and men.
